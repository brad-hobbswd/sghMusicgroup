Services We Offer

Radio Promotion

For 4 months you will get radio coverage internationally as well as 1 featured ad in Christian Voice Magazine and Cashbox Magazine. We will send out releases every 4 months (January, April, July, October as well as a Christmas Release) to all charting and non charting stations.

Concert Promotions

By Handpicking our Artists, we host Special Events in different areas like Ohio, West Virginia, Tennessee, Kentucky, South Carolina, North Carolina and would love an opportunity to come to your area and minister. SGH Music Group provides booking opportunities whether it be for a Worship Service, Homecoming, Benefit Singing, Conventions, and Showcases. Our goal is and always has been reaching the lost through Southern Gospel Music.

Popular Question

How can we get SGH Music Group or one of their artists to our event or church service? You can give us a call and we can connect you with the right artist for your event. We do believe in taking care of our artists and request a Love Offering to be taken to assist with the cost of travel.
